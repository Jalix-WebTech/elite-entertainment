Who We Are

Redefining Entertainment Excellence

Founded on the principles of passion, professionalism, and unmatched creativity, Elite Entertainment is a premier music and event entertainment company based in Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria.

We specialize in delivering world-class live performances, full-scale event production, and premium entertainment services — transforming every occasion into a memorable, show-stopping experience.

From intimate weddings to massive concerts, church programs to corporate galas, our multidisciplinary team delivers tailored entertainment solutions that exceed expectations.

Meet Our CEO

Francis Edem

Founder · Creative Director · Music Producer

A visionary entertainer and music producer with over two decades of distinguished practice, our CEO founded Elite Entertainment with a singular mission: to redefine the African entertainment landscape through world-class performances and event production.

An accomplished musician, band leader, and event strategist, the CEO brings a rare synthesis of creative genius, technical mastery, and business acumen. Having produced landmark events across West Africa — from sold-out concerts to state-level celebrations — each engagement is a testament to entertainment's power to unite communities.

Music is not merely sound — it is the language of the human soul. At Elite Entertainment, we don't just perform at events; we create moments that echo in hearts forever, bridging cultures and igniting joy across our great continent.
